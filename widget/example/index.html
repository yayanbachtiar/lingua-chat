🏡 My Website

Welcome to my website! This is a demo page showing how LinguaChat can be embedded as a floating chat widget — just like Intercom or Crisp.

The chat bubble in the bottom-right corner was added with just one line of code.

⚡ How to embed

Add this before </body> on your page:

<iframe src="http://localhost:8001"
  style="position:fixed;bottom:20px;right:20px;
         width:380px;height:600px;border:none;
         border-radius:12px;z-index:9999;">
</iframe>
